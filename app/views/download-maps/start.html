AR FIELDING Get a map of your land

Use this service to get a map of your land.

You can use this service to:

  • get maps of your whole holding or specific land parcels
  • download a printable map
  • save maps to your device

You can choose the information your maps display. For example, land cover, areas with live agreements.

You cannot use this service to change land details. For guidance on how to do this, read Digital maps in the Rural Payments service: check and change your maps .
