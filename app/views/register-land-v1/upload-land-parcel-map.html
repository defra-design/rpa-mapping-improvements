AR FIELDING Upload land parcel map

Upload an annotated map of the land parcel. For guidance see Creating a sketch map to register land or update digital maps.

The name of your sketch map must be:

  • be unique
  • be no longer than 20 characters
  • contain only letters or numbers (no spaces)

We can only accept sketch maps that are:

  • 10MB or less
  • in an accepted format (jpg, jpeg, png, pdf, doc or docx)
