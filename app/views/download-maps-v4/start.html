AR FIELDING Get maps of your land

Use this service to get:

  • maps of your whole holding or specific land parcels
  • information about your land's size, features, and live agreements

Once you've selected the information you need and what your maps should display you can:

  • download printable versions
  • save them to your device

You cannot use this service to change land details. For guidance on how to do this, read Digital maps in the Rural Payments service: check and change your maps .
