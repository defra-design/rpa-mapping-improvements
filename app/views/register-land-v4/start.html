AR FIELDING Register land

Use this service to register land to your business. This can be:

  • new land
  • land that does not have a parcel ID yet
  • land you have bought or taken over management of

Before you start

Read the guidance Register rural land on the Rural Payments service.

For land you want to register you will need either:

  • a parcel ID
  • details of the land's location

You need to know the date the change applies from. To register changes that happened more than 12 months ago, complete an RLE1 form instead.

If the land is new or has never been registered, you might need to upload a map to show us the change. We will tell you if you need to do this.
